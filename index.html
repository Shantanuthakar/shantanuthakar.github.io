I am a PhD student advised by Dr. Satyandra K. Gupta at the Mechanical Engineering Department in the University of Southern California. I work in the Realization of Robotic Systems Laboratory based out of the Center for Advanced Manufacturing at USC. My research interests include robotic motion and trajectory planning, task planning, machine learning, robot dynamics and control.

The applications I am currently working on include trajectory planning of mobile manipulators, high speed grasping of parts, point-to-point path and trajectory generation of high degree of freedom systems. I have recently started working in task planning in high DOF systems.
